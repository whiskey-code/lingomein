# lingomein
Public terms and conditions and privacy policy for LingoMeIn app

Last Updated: February 2026
Developer: Whiskey‑Code (Clint)
1. Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how this app (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information. The App provides real‑time translation, text‑to‑speech, and headset‑based language tools. We respect your privacy and only collect the minimum information needed for the App to function.
2. Information We Collect
A. Information You Provide
• 	Text you type into the app (for translation or text‑to‑speech)
• 	Language selections and preferences
This information is processed only to perform translations and is not stored after the translation is completed.
B. Automatically Collected Information
• 	Device type and operating system
• 	App usage statistics (e.g., number of translations, feature usage)
• 	Crash logs and diagnostics (to improve performance)
No personal identity information is collected.
C. Audio Processing
If you use voice translation:
• 	Your audio is sent securely to our translation provider
• 	It is processed in real time
• 	It is not stored after the translation is completed
We do not keep recordings.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
• 	Provide translations and text‑to‑speech
• 	Improve app performance
• 	Maintain security and prevent abuse
• 	Understand feature usage to improve the app
We do not sell your data.
We do not share your data with advertisers.
4. Third‑Party Services
The App uses trusted third‑party services for:
• 	Translation
• 	Text‑to‑speech
• 	Analytics
• 	Crash reporting
These services only receive the data required to perform their function and are required to protect it.

5. Data Retention
We do not store:
• 	Audio recordings
• 	Text you translate
• 	Conversations
• 	Personal identifiers
Temporary data is deleted automatically after processing.
6. Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security
We use industry‑standard encryption and secure communication protocols to protect your data during transmission.

8. Your Choices
You may:
• 	Disable microphone access
• 	Delete the app at any time
• 	Decline analytics sharing (if your device supports it)
9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
